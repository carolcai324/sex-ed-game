Rules of the Game

  1. Pick a figurine and place it by the by the start line.
  2. Roll the 4 sided dice to decide who goes first.
  3. Now the game starts. first player roll the dice and move according to the number.
  4. Based on the shape you land on, pick up a card randomly (check the key for which category to pick) and try to answer the question.
  5. If you get the answer right, proceed one extra step, but if you get it wrong, reverse one step.
  6. The player who reaches the end line first wins.
